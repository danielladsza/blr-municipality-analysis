# blr-municipality-analysis
Analysis of 16,071 citizen-reported civic complaints in Bangalore (2019–2022) using Excel and Canva
BLR Municipality Issue Log — Data Analysis
Objective
To analyse citizen-reported civic complaints in Bangalore from 2019 to 2022 and identify key trends in issue types and resolution rates.
Dataset
Source: Open City India — I Change My City dataset
16,071 complaints across 4 years
Key Findings

Only 30.7% of complaints were ever resolved
Roads & footpaths and garbage account for over 56% of all complaints
Yellow Spot complaints have a near-zero resolution rate of 2.3%
Complaints dropped year-on-year, likely due to reporting fatigue

Tools Used
Excel, Canva
