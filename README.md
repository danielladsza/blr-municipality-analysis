# BLR Municipality Issue Log — Data Analysis

## Objective
To analyse citizen-reported civic complaints in Bangalore from 2019 to 2022 
and identify key trends in issue types and resolution rates.

## Dataset
- **Source:** Open City India — I Change My City
- **Size:** 16,071 complaints across 4 years (2019–2022)

## Key Findings
- Only **30.7%** of complaints were ever resolved
- Roads & footpaths and garbage account for over **56%** of all complaints
- Yellow Spot complaints have a near-zero resolution rate of **2.3%**
- Complaints dropped year-on-year, likely due to reporting fatigue
